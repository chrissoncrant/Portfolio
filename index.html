A Bit About Me

I graduated from CSU, Sacramento with a B.A. in Communications and since then I have spent several years working in the tech support/customer service industry.

Feeling burnt-out with this work I began searching for something new, something that would challenge me while playing on my strengths.

The search led me to the world of coding and software development. I believe whole-heartedly that I have found what I have been looking for and am excited for what awaits me as I continue down this promising path.

I feel fortunate to have come across Thinkful's Immersive Engineering course as I believe it will help me hone the necessary skills needed to thrive in this industry.

One part of this journey I am especially excited about is to meet other future and current developers along the way, building meaningful relationships while doing interesting work!

When I am not coding, I spend a good deal of time playing guitar and dabbling in musical composition. I am finding that music and coding are quite synergetic!
