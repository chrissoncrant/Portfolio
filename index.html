A Bit About Me

I graduated from CSU, Sacramento with a B.A. in Communications in 2016. Since then, I have been working in the tech support/customer service industry.

Feeling burnt-out by this gig, I began searching for something new. I knew I wanted to find something that would challenge me while playing on my strengths.

The search led me to the world of coding and software development, and, let me tell you, this is what I've been searching for!

I feel fortunate that I found Thinkful's Immersive Engineering course. I believe it will be crucial in helping me to hone the necessary skills needed to thrive in this industry.

I am excited to meet and work with other future and current developers along the way. I believe that the combination of building meaningful relationships while doing interesting work is priceless.

When not coding, I love to play guitar and dabble in musical composition. And I am finding that music and coding are wonderfully synergistic!
